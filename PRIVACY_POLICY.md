# JobForm AutoFill Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 6, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
JobForm AutoFill ("the Extension") collects and stores the personal information, demographic data, and document files (e.g., Resume, Cover Letter) that you explicitly provide via the Extension's Options page.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
The sole purpose of collecting this information is to automatically fill out job application web forms on your behalf when you click the "Auto-Fill Form" button. 

## 3. Data Storage and Security
**All data is stored locally on your device.** The Extension uses Chrome's native `chrome.storage.local` API to save your profile data and files. 

*   We **do not** transmit your data to any external servers.
*   We **do not** use analytics, telemetry, or tracking scripts.
*   Your data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly choose to submit a web form that the Extension has populated.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We **do not** sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information or files with any third parties.

## 5. User Control
You have full control over your data. You can view, modify, or permanently delete all saved information and files at any time by clicking the "Clear Data" button on the Extension's Options page. Uninstalling the extension will also permanently remove the locally stored data.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
If we make changes to how we handle your data, we will update this Privacy Policy. Since we do not collect your email address, we encourage you to review this policy periodically on our GitHub repository.

## 7. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository: [https://github.com/joelstransky/job-form-filler](https://github.com/joelstransky/job-form-filler)
